It is not a grant of permission to commercially exploit the Tracewright concept, name, methodology, starter kit, dashboard, prompts, templates, or distinctive review structure.

## Allowed Without Prior Permission

- personal document review
- classroom or workshop use without selling Tracewright as a product
- academic research
- non-commercial writing, editing, and revision support
- testing the Starter Kit with a personal AI assistant
- adapting the files for private, non-commercial local use

- selling Tracewright-based tools, dashboards, prompts, workflows, or services

For commercial collaboration, licensing, institutional use, or product integration, please contact Ayako Redon before using the project.
